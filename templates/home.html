What Could You Do?

Kenect has two different apps, the DJ interface and the mobile app for users. Let's take a look at both applications.
DJs

DJs are enabled to login to our DJ interface. They're able to turn their shift on and start collecting revenue for playing music.

  • Accept Song Requests

    When a user requests a song, it will display in the DJs home interface. They can choose to accept the song or not.

  • Receive Income

    If they accept the song, it will bill the user and add the income into the DJs account. They can withdraw their income every night.

Partygoers

When a partygoer arrives at the venue, the app will connect them to the DJ and they can quickly request their favorite songs for a small price.

  • Request Songs

    The partygoer can easily request their favorite song within seconds.

  • Memories

    Film your memories at the party that night and upload it to the venues page. It'll last for 24 hours unless you do a ever-lasting memory.
